Who am I?

I’m a 21-year-old premed who’s a Computer Science major in the United States. I currently commute from home, and I scored a job offer that I’ll be pursuing after graduation. I’m planning on taking the MCAT this summer and applying next year to medical school.

About Me

  1. Why am a Computer Science major? Background Information.
    I didn’t want to be a science major and do Biology or Chemistry for 4 years. I heard Dance majors or any major can get into medical school as long as the science GPA is high. So, I picked a nonconventional major that would also double as a backup plan in case I didn’t get into medical school. Since I’m mathematically oriented, Computer Science seemed right. Years later, and I see the benefits of this major, so much so that I even thought of just rolling with Computer Science only. BUT I do want to see if I can merge medicine and Computer Science in the future if I get a medical degree.
